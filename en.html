Signature Dishes

Hot Stone Bibimbap

A Korean staple! Fragrant sesame oil and plenty of vegetables, finished healthily with additive-free seasoning.

Seafood & Scallion Pancake

Crispy outside, chewy inside. Made without eggs to let the original flavors of the ingredients shine.

Soft Tofu Stew
